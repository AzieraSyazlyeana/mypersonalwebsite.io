HELLO!

The table below shows my education journey. When I was in form 4 and form 5, I took science stream subjects where I had to study additional subjects such as Addmath, Physics, Chemistry and Engineering Drawing

My SPM result is 5A. Among them, A is the subject of Engineering Drawing. Because of that, I decided to continue my studies at UiTM in the Building Surveying course related to engineering drawings

My semester in Building Survey cost is 6 semesters which is approximately 3 years. While I was there, I was active in the co-curriculum of the Malaysian Civil Defense Association. I became a member of the guild, and got the position of commander.

I was able to study face-to-face at UiTM for only 3 semesters, because at that time the Covid-19 pandemic hit Malaysia causing everyone to be quarantined at home. Therefore, I underwent online learning for 2 semesters, and underwent an internship at the Taiping Municipal Council in Taiping, Perak in 2021

After I finished my internship, I continued to apply for the UiTM application and chose the library management course. I chose library because I want to try new things and get new experiences and I want to stay away from the world of engineering because that subject is quite difficult for me.
